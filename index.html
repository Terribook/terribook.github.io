Terribook was founded in the 5th grade classroom of CAS Mrs. Conley's class of 2012.

It all started out with a poster and sticky notes, but now its slowly progressing.

Terribook can also be seen as a Kateri's twist of a social media network,

Kateri and Facebook.

After the concept was thought out by Kateri, a poster was drawn, perhaps a 3x3 sq. ft. with the edges rounded by scissors and the skilled hands of 5th grade artist in the classroom.
It was themed such as this website, a clear sky blue with cartoonish white clouds.
